Vibrato

What is it and how we produce it

what is vibrato?

Vibrato is a musical effect that consists of a regular, pulsating change of pitch. Vibrato can be produced using vocal techniques, string and wind instruments, and mechanical and digital devices.

Note that while the terms vibrato and tremolo are sometimes used interchangibly, vibrato defined as the periodic variation in pitch while tremolo is the fast repitition of the same note.
