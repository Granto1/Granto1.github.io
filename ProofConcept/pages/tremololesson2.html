Tremolo

What is it and how we produce it

what is tremolo?

In music, tremolo is a trembling effect produced either through the rapid repitition of of a note or a variation in note volume. The rapid reiteration of a single note is a characteristic effect of bowed string instruments, while amplitude variation is more characteristic of organs and electric guitars.
