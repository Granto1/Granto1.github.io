Vibrato

What is Vibrato?

Vibrato is a musical effect that consists of pulsating changes in pitch.  

How is Vibrato Implemented?

For this synthesizer, a phase shift is added to the sinusoidal functions for which each waveform is based upon. As such,  you are able to adjust the depth and oscillating frequency.  Try it for yourself!

Adjust the slider and click the button or click "a" on your keyboard!
